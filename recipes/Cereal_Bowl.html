Literally Just a Bowl of Cereal

Description of Dish:

A core part of breakfast; this dish is literally just your favorite cereal poured into a bowl and covered in milk.

Best eaten with a spoon.

Ingredients:

  • 1-2 cups of milk
  • a box or bag of your preferred breakfast cereal
  • a bowl
  • a spoon (optional)

Steps for Preparing Dish:

  1. Pour enough cereal into the bowl to fill it roughly 3/4 of the way up.
  2. Pour milk into bowl, over cereal. It is of paramount importance that the milk goes in after the cereal.
  3. If using a spoon to consume the meal, deposit the spoon into the bowl now, and begin eating.
