Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description of Dish:

Warm, gooey and melty cheddar cheese sandwiched between two perfectly crisped slices of white bread.

Best paired with a bowl of tomato soup.

Ingredients:

  • 1 TBSP of Mayonnaise
  • 2 slices of preferred bread
  • 2 oz. of cheddar cheese

Steps for Preparing Dish:

  1. Spread one slice of bread with mayonnaise. Place bread mayonnaise side down into a hot skillet. Add cheese onto face of bread.
  2. Spread one side of other piece of bread with mayonnaise. Place this piece mayonnaise side up onto cheese.
  3. Cook until bottom side of bottom piece of bread is browned. Flip sandwich. Continue cooking until cheese is melty and both slices of bread are browned.
